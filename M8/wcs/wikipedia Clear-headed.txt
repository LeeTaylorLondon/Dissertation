Clear Headed is a perk for Invention that lengthens Anticipate's stun immunity, but completely removes its damage reduction. It can be created in weapon and armour gizmos.
patch 7 March 2016 (Update):The Clearheaded perk will no longer stop Anticipation appearing on your opposition's target interface in PvP.
