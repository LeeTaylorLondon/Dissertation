On 31 May 2022 it was reported that Missguided had gone into administration after being unable to pay its debts.[13] The next day, on 1 June 2022, it was announced that Frasers Group had bought the intellectual property of Missguided and its sister brand Mennace for around £20 million.[14] It is estimated that 87 staff members will be made redundant and 147 will be moved to Frasers Group,[15] with the legal name changed to MGL Realisations (2022) Limited in July 2022.[16]
