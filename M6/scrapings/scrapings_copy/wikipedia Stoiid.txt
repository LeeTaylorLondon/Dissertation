Since 2003, Marcus H has used an internet approach to the promotion of his music project Soiled. Releasing music via Elm Lodge Records, Bizarre, Electronic Musik, Dirty Patches. His music has featured on US College Radio Kalx,[2] Huw Stephens' BBC Radio 1 show,[3] BBC Radio 6 Music Freakzone and BBC Radio Tees celebration of The Beatles album Revolver, where he covered "Yellow Submarine".[4]

Soiled is an English experimental, electro-minimal cinematographic music project, created in 2002 by Marcus H. He has released five albums, numerous EPs, collaborations and compilation tracks.
