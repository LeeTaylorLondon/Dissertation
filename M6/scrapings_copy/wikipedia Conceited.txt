Conceited has appeared on King of the Dot, Ultimate Rap League, and Don't Flop in the US, on Got Beef? in Australia, and on O-Zone Battles in Sweden.[citation needed] His battle against Dumbfoundead at KOTD's "Blackout 5" event in Toronto received more than 2.6 million views in less than six months, and was the second most viewed English language battle.[citation needed]
He also had his own sneakers show on MTV called Sneaker Wars,[1] and co-hosted the 2018 YouTube revival of Singled Out alongside Justina Valentine.[2]
